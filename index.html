Manage Your Crypto Safely with Ledger Live Desktop

Ledger Live Desktop is the official desktop application from Ledger that empowers you to securely manage, stake, and grow your digital assets. Designed for performance and protection, it connects directly with your Ledger hardware wallet, keeping your private keys offline while you enjoy seamless access to your portfolio.

Key Features of Ledger Live Desktop

Ledger Live Desktop offers a unified interface for managing your entire crypto portfolio. Whether you're a beginner or advanced investor, this app provides the tools and confidence to transact securely.

All-in-One Dashboard

View balances, transactions, and asset performance at a glance — all from one sleek interface.

Secure Transactions

With Ledger Live Desktop, all crypto transactions are verified through your Ledger hardware wallet, ensuring your private keys never leave the device.

Staking & Swaps

Stake coins and swap assets directly within Ledger Live Desktop without compromising your security.
